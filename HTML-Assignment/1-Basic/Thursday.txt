	<h1>Thursday</h1>
			
	<p>
	<strong>Ch 44 Surah Ad-Dukhan (The Smoke)</strong>
	</p>

	<p>
	It is narrated from the Holy Prophet (S) that if this Surah is recited at night, then seventy thousand angels pray to Allah (S.w.T.) to forgive the sins of the reciter. If recited on <b>Thursday nights,</b> 
	<em>all sins are forgiven and houses are built for the reciter in Jannah.</em>
	</p>
