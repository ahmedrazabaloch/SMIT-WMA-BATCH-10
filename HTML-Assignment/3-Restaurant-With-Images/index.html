SERVED EVERY DAY SINCE 1995

Betak opened on Thanksgiving Day 1995. Chef / Owner Ahmedraza began baking pies and selling them to restaurants and his neighbors out of a small kitchen at the corner of Hudson and North Moore St. in Tribeca. Today, lalokheti beloved restaurant and pie shop celebrates 27 years of classic, made from scratch Balochi cooking.
