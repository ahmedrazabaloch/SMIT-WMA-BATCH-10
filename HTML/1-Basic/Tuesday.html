Tuesday

Allah Almighty created diseases on Tuesday. He descended Iblees on earth on this day, created hell, appointed the angel of death on the souls of humans, Qabeel murdered Habeel, Hazrat Moosa and Haroon – peace be upon Them, passed away and Hazrat Ayyoob – peace be upon Him – was put through a test through disease on this day.

(Faid Al-Qadeer Sharah Jame’ Sagheer Vol. 1, p. 47)
